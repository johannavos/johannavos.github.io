Research Interests

I am interested in understanding the atmospheres of brown dwarfs and giant exoplanets. Young brown dwarfs act as powerful analogs to the directly-imaged exoplanets, since they have identical temperatures, masses and ages. They can be observed in exquisite detail using current facilities since they are isolated objects. They offer powerful insights on what to expect from the directly-imaged exoplanets.
